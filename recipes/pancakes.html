Pancakes

Description

These pancake are perfect for pancake day!

Ingredients

  • milk
  • flour
  • oil
  • egg
  • salt

Steps

  1. First, mix all the ingredients together
  2. heat oil in pan
  3. pour mix in pan
  4. wait for bubbles to appear, then flip
  5. cook on the other side, then enjoy with lemon and sugar!
